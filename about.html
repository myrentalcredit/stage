Building Credit BY Reporting Your Payments

Started to serve and help everyone who pays their rent and wants credit for it.

My Rental Credit was founded on the belief that renters should have the same benefit from paying their monthly rent payments as mortgage holders paying their monthly mortgage payments. Monthly payments are contracts, no matter the type of contract, shouldn’t your receive your credit?

We are committed to providing an easy, simple service for renters that simply adds their monthly rental payments to their credit reports and as a result improves their credit score and allows them to reach their financial goals.  Pay your rent online and build your credit.

My Rental Credits will report your payments to Transunion credit agency.
