Frequently asked questions

How long will it take for my score to change?

On average, customers see a 35-50 point change in their credit score within the first 15 days.

Can I get my money back?

Guaranteed sign up fee and first month’s subscription refund if you are not satisfied within the first 45 days. If you are not satisfied after 45 days, you will be refunded the last month’s subscription that was not reported to the credit bureaus.

Why have a sign up fee & monthly subscriptions?

The one-time sign up fee of 49.95 or 69.95 covers the initial landlord verification fee and reporting up to two years of rental payment history to the credit bureaus. The monthly subscription fee of 9.95 allows MyRentalCredit to automatically verify and continuously report your ongoing rent payments.

How is my landlord involved in this process?

To prevent any fraud, we need to independently verify the identity of your landlord, who then verifies tenant and rent payment history information. This is what enables us to take the next step of reporting your 2-year history and ongoing rent payment to the credit bureaus.

It is very rare that a landlord or property manager is unwilling to work with us. If that happens, we will be unable to verify and report your rent history and will provide you with a full refund within 30 days.

Why and when is a rent verification letter important?

A rent verification letter is an important document that verifies the history of your on-time rent payments. This letter can be used for future landlords to evaluate you as a potential tenant. In addition, this letter can effect future credit transactions.

How much will my credit score increase?

Your credit score will increase with the continuous reporting of your monthly on time rent payments. The verification of on-time payments will help build your credit.

How does rent reporting work?

There are 4 simple steps to the rent reporting process:

1.     Sign up and verify information. Fill in the required information that includes: name, address, date of birth, social security number and your landlord’s contact information. Your social security number is used to match your credit profile.
 
2.     Verify landlord and rent payments.  The landlord must first be verified based on the provided information. He/or she then verifies the tenant and rent payment history. 

3.     Report Rent Payments to Credit BureausMyRentalCredit reports your rent history to Transunion, one of the major credit bureaus.  Upon completion you will receive an email. Experian and Equifax will also be used for reporting in the near future.
  
4.     New Tradeline on Credit HistoryWithin 5-7 business days, a new credit line will appear on your credit report.  Once the new credit line appears on your credit report, you will receive an email. It is important to keep your subscription paid so that the credit bureau can continue to add information to your tradeline. 

5.     Is MyRetalCredit a credit repair service?We are not a credit repair service. We provide you with a simple and effective way to quickly improve your credit score based on past and future rent payments.
